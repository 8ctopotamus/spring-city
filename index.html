MISSION

The mission of the Spring City Garden Club is to share our love of gardening through beautification, environmental stewardship, education, community involvement and philanthropy.

Guests and new members are always welcome.

Events

Our annual Plant, Yard Art, Shabby Chic, and Bakery Sale held the first Saturday after Memorial Day at the E.B. Shurts building (corner of Prairie and College Avenues) is well respected in the community and attendance is always paramount for members to provide plants and planting expertise.
