MISSION

The mission of the Spring City Garden Club is to share our love of gardening through beautification, environmental stewardship, education, community involvement and philanthropy.


More information

Show me more

Community Involvement

Planting the downtown Waukesha planters with summer flowers and winter interest is a project enjoyed by downtown businesses and visitors.

Environmental Stewardship

The club supports the Waukesha County Dept. of Parks and Land Use "Legacy Forest Program" by planting at least two annual trees each year.

Education

Our monthly speakers, field trips and/or overnight trips allow the members to increase their knowledge of our world.

Philanthropy

Philanthropic projects that benefit the environment and the community.

Events & Sales

Our annual Plant, Yard Art, Shabby Chic, and Bakery Sale held the first Saturday after Memorial Day at the E.B. Shurts building (corner of Prairie and College Avenues) is well respected in the community and member participation in our plant sale by providing plants and plant expertise is paramount to our success.
